Our History

This coffee shop serves various types of coffee, latte, glace, cappuccino etc. It has an espresso bar that specializes in serving espresso and espresso based drinks.

From a cultural standpoint, it serves as a center of social interaction. It is a place where people meet to congregate, talk, write, entertain one another, or pass time , whether individually or in groups.

This coffee shop serves as an informal club for its regular members. As early as the 1950s Beatnik era and the 1960s folk music scene, coffee houses have hosted singer-songwriter performances , typically in the evening.
